Announcments

It is with great pleasure we announce two wonderful opportunities to start our return back to normalcy at church on Sundays

Starting Sunday, February 21st singing (with masks still on) will be allowed in our meetings. We will be blessed as we return to a wonderful form of worship as we sing, opening and closing hymns, as well as the Sacrament hymn.

We will also move back into 2nd hour classes offered at the church building. Please watch for more specific details to be released next week, but please plan on the following: Sacrament meeting will be from 10:00-10:50am The ward will still be divided in half alphbetically for attendance every other week 2nd hour classes will be held from 11:00-11:30am Classes will be available for adults, youth as well as primary

We are trying to get on a fairly set schedule for youth nights. They will be held on the 1st and 3rd Tuesdays of each month starting at 7pm. We encourage families to support their youth in attending on a regular basis.

For those unaware, the 2021 Trek has been canceled by the church. We are planning on having a stay at home ward youth conference June 10-12th so mark your calendars for those dates.

Activities (Mark your calendars)

Tuesday, February 16th we will be holding a combined YM/YW Valentine's Dinner at the church starting at 7pm. The meal will be a catered "Chipotle" night served by the Bishopric. Please encourage your youth to attend.

We are happy to announce that we will be holding a Family Fun Night at Pirate's Cove on Monday, July 19th from 6:30-8:30pm. Mark your calendars and come enjoy a night of swimming, slides and tubing as a ward family.
